# Restreamer

> datarhei Restreamer is a powerful video streaming platform designed to provide real-time live video streaming on websites and social media platforms. It eliminates the need for additional streaming provider costs for video hosting or software licensing. The app allows teams to live stream and restream everything from webcams to broadcasting, making streaming more efficient and user-friendly. It offers live streaming on websites without additional service providers or video hosting providers, and it's instantly available, free of charge, and without advertising. Users can share live videos publicly or privately with anyone, or use a secret link to give their audience access to private content. The app also provides a simple streaming server with self-installation on your server or Raspberry Pi. The fully documented API makes datarhei Restreamer easy to adapt to any project.
