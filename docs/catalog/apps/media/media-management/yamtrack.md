# Yamtrack

> Yamtrack is a self-hosted media tracking application designed to help you keep track of your movies, TV shows, books, video games, and other media consumption. It provides a clean, modern interface for logging what you're watching, reading, or playing, along with ratings, reviews, and personal notes. Yamtrack features automatic metadata fetching from popular databases, progress tracking, viewing statistics, and customizable lists. The application supports multiple media types and offers detailed analytics about your consumption habits. Built with a focus on privacy and simplicity, Yamtrack keeps all your tracking data under your control. Perfect for media enthusiasts who want to maintain a comprehensive log of their entertainment without relying on third-party services.
