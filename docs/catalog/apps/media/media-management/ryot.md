# Ryot

> Ryot is a self-hosted platform for tracking and managing your media consumption across movies, TV shows, books, anime, manga, video games, podcasts, and more. Designed as an all-in-one solution for media enthusiasts, Ryot lets you maintain a comprehensive record of everything you've watched, read, played, or listened to. The application provides powerful tracking features including progress monitoring, ratings, reviews, and custom lists to organize your media library. With integrations for popular platforms like Trakt, Goodreads, and various media databases, Ryot can automatically fetch metadata, cover images, and detailed information for your media entries. The clean, modern interface works seamlessly across devices, allowing you to update your progress from anywhere. Key features include import capabilities from other tracking services, detailed statistics and insights about your media consumption habits, calendar views for upcoming releases, and recommendation suggestions based on your history. Built with performance in mind, Ryot uses PostgreSQL for reliable data storage and provides a fast, responsive experience. Perfect for media enthusiasts who want complete control over their tracking data, users migrating from commercial tracking services who want privacy and ownership, collectors who want to catalog their media libraries, or anyone who enjoys keeping detailed records of their entertainment consumption without relying on third-party platforms.
