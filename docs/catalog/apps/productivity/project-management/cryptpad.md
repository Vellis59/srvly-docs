# Cryptpad

> CryptPad is an open-source collaborative office suite that offers end-to-end encryption, ensuring your data remains private and secure. It provides a full suite of applications, allowing you to work on various projects in real-time with your colleagues or friends. Whether you're planning a camping trip or setting up client briefs, CryptPad makes it easy to share files and collaborate without the need for account creation or app downloads. It's more than just a document editor; it's a secure platform for all your collaborative needs.
