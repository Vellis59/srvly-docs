# Nametag

> Nametag is a simple, yet effective Personal Relationship Manager (PRM) - like a CRM, but for your actual relationships instead of sales prospects. Built with Next.js, it helps you manage your personal network by tracking contacts, mapping relationships between people, and visualizing your network with interactive graphs. Remember when you last talked to an old friend, their kids' names, or their birthday. Organize contacts into custom groups, set reminders for important dates and staying in touch, and keep all your relationship data in one secure, self-hosted location.
