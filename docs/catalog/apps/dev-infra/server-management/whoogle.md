# Whoogle

> Whoogle is an open-source, privacy-focused search engine that serves as a proxy for Google Search. It allows users to access Google’s powerful search capabilities without being tracked, monitored, or bombarded with ads. Whoogle removes trackers, logs, and ads, ensuring that search queries and browsing activities remain private and anonymous. Users benefit from Google's accurate and comprehensive search results, while maintaining full control over their personal data. Its lightweight design makes it easy to deploy and use, often preferred by those seeking to balance search efficiency with enhanced privacy.
